# Review Checklist

Use this checklist mentally before returning a revised chapter.

## Retention-critical checks
- The first paragraph enters pressure fast.
- The chapter goal is visible within the first screen.
- There is at least one concrete reversal, gain, or reveal.
- The last paragraph pushes the next click.

## Human-sounding prose checks
- Fewer explanation labels, more action or reaction.
- Dialogue lines are shorter and character-specific.
- Repeated connector words are reduced.
- Paragraph rhythm is varied.

## Continuity checks
- Names, titles, realms, timeline, and faction relations remain consistent.
- The revised chapter still lands the intended outline beat.
- No accidental power inflation or dropped causal link.
