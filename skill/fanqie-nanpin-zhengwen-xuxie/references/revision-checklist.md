# Revision checklist

Use this checklist after drafting and before returning the polished version.

## Continuity
- Does the first scene begin where the prior chapter plausibly ended?
- Are names, titles, injuries, levels, and relationships consistent?
- Does the chapter complete at least one visible part of the chapter goal?

## Fanqie male-frequency pacing
- Is there a hook in the first few paragraphs?
- Is there at least one tangible gain, danger spike, or revelation?
- Does the ending create immediate curiosity?

## Anti-AI polish
- Cut repeated recap.
- Replace abstract emotional statements with concrete scene behavior.
- Shorten bloated transitional phrases.
- Make dialogue asymmetrical and character-led.
- Remove essay-style explanation where a line of action would do better.

## Genre reminders
### Xuanhuan leveling
- Track cultivation cost and resource logic.

### Historical conquest
- Track command chain and political consequence.

### Urban high-concept
- Keep language sharp and payoff visible.
