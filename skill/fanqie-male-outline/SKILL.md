# Fanqie Male Outline

## Overview
Build commercially oriented outlines for番茄男频 stories. Default to **outline-first planning**, not polished prose. The skill should convert a user's **题材 + 卖点 + 目标字数** into a scalable story plan that can support daily serialization and strong reader retention.

Prioritize these subgenres:
- 玄幻升级
- 历史争霸
- 都市脑洞

When the request is vague, infer the most commercial variant of the premise instead of asking many follow-up questions.

## Working Principles

### 1. Write for retention, not literary elegance
Optimize for:
- clear protagonist advantage
- immediate conflict in the opening
- visible power growth or status growth
- repeated payoff loops
- chapter-end hooks
- room for long serialization

Avoid:
- slow atmospheric openings
- overly abstract worldbuilding before conflict
- passive protagonists
- too many names or factions too early
- early chapters that only explain setting

### 2. Keep the outline commercially legible
Every outline should make these things obvious:
- who the protagonist is
- what makes him different
- what he wants right now
- what blocks him
- what the next payoff is
- how the story can keep escalating

### 3. Treat the first 50 chapters as the true launch window
The first 50 chapters must:
- establish the core hook fast
- complete at least one strong mini-arc
- widen the world after initial payoff
- show clear upward momentum
- leave enough unresolved tension for continued serialization

## Workflow

### Step 1: Classify the premise
Map the user's idea into one of the three focus lanes.

#### 玄幻升级
Default emphasis:
- talent gap or humiliation opening
- cheat, inheritance, simulator, treasure, special constitution, or hidden identity
- combat progression, sect conflict, resource争夺, rank breakthrough
- recurring face-slapping and stage-based advancement

#### 历史争霸
Default emphasis:
-乱世,穿越,边军,县城,流民,门阀,权臣,皇权,经营,征战
- protagonist starts with weak political/military resources but unusual insight or system-like edge
- progression alternates between survival, recruitment, territory control, faction struggle, and expansion
- strategy must stay readable; do not bury the outline in dense historical detail

#### 都市脑洞
Default emphasis:
- modern relatable setting
- one outrageous but easy-to-understand hook
- visible life upgrade, class leap, revenge, or hidden identity payoff
- business, school, workplace, city power games, livestreaming, medicine, appraisal, technology, or supernatural urban overlays

If the premise could fit multiple lanes, choose the lane with the strongest **continuous payoff engine**.

### Step 2: Distill the commercial core
Before drafting the outline, explicitly derive:
- **一句话卖点**
- **主角核心优势**
- **开局困境**
- **首个阶段目标**
- **长期升级主线**
- **可重复爽点机制**

If these are weak, strengthen them before continuing.

### Step 3: Choose an outline scale from target word count
Use the target word count to decide how large the skeleton should feel.

- **80万字以下**: tighter arc density, fewer major factions, faster payoff
- **80万-150万字**: standard commercial longform, room for several regions/stages/factions
- **150万字以上**: build a broader ladder of advancement, but keep early arcs simple and addictive

No matter the total target, always provide a detailed **first 50 chapters**.

### Step 4: Build the story engine
Design the story so it can keep producing chapters.

Every usable outline needs:
- a core conflict engine
- a growth engine
- a relationship engine
- an external pressure engine
- a reveal engine

Examples:
- 玄幻升级: advancement + sect rivalry + treasure competition + hidden bloodline reveals
- 历史争霸: survival + governance + recruitment + enemy encirclement + court politics
- 都市脑洞: public humiliation reversal + wealth/identity growth + new circles + stronger opponents + secret exposure risk

### Step 5: Produce the deliverable
Default output should include the sections below in this order unless the user requested a shorter format.

## Default Output Format

### A. 定位结论
State:
- 题材归类
- 目标读者爽点
- 核心连载引擎
- 适合番茄的原因

### B. 一句话卖点
Write 1 strong version and 2 backup versions.

### C. 故事总纲
Include:
- 主角
- 开局处境
- 金手指/核心优势
- 主线目标
- 中期扩张方向
- 长线终局方向

### D. 全书结构建议
Split the novel into major stages according to target word count.
For each stage, include:
- stage objective
- main conflict
- power/status gain
- next escalation gate

### E. 前50章细纲
Organize in blocks of 10 chapters:
- 第1-10章
- 第11-20章
- 第21-30章
- 第31-40章
- 第41-50章

For **each chapter**, include all of the following:
- chapter number
- chapter title
- core event
- protagonist gain or setback
- chapter-end hook

Keep each chapter compact but concrete. Do not write empty filler beats.

### F. 风格选择说明
Briefly state which style the skill selected and why.
Use one primary mode:
- 爽点密集，节奏快
- 稳定长线，适合日更
- 悬念强，章尾钩子重
- 更像真人作者，尽量去 ai 味

Choose based on the premise, not randomly.

## Style Selection Rules

### 爽点密集，节奏快
Use when:
- 都市脑洞
- 明牌打脸
- 快速升级
- 强系统文

Behavior:
- faster reversals
- shorter setup
- more frequent payoffs
- cleaner enemies and clearer humiliations

### 稳定长线，适合日更
Use when:
- 长篇玄幻升级
- 历史争霸经营
- 多阶段成长线

Behavior:
- more room for subgoals
- stronger stage transitions
- sustainable escalation
- avoid blowing all cards in 20 chapters

### 悬念强，章尾钩子重
Use when:
- 阴谋线重
- 历史权谋
- 都市异能悬疑
- 副本/秘密推进

Behavior:
- each chapter should end with a reveal, threat, countdown, or unexpected identity move
- keep information release controlled

### 更像真人作者，尽量去 ai 味
Use when:
- user explicitly asks for it
- premise depends on tone consistency or stronger lived-in detail

Behavior:
- reduce repetitive formula language
- vary hook types
- keep character motives specific
- avoid generic “震惊全场” style phrasing unless the market logic clearly needs it

## Subgenre-Specific Guidance

### 玄幻升级
Mandatory elements:
- visible cultivation or combat ladder
- humiliation, suppression, scarcity, or elimination pressure early
- a cheat that solves some problems but creates bigger exposure or competition
- at least 3 meaningful advancement gates within the early structure

Good early engines:
- outer sect to inner sect
- trial and inheritance competition
- clan crisis to regional expansion
- special physique awakening under public contempt

### 历史争霸
Mandatory elements:
- immediate survival pressure
- starting territory or power base, however small
- recruitment of at least 2 memorable helper figures early
- one early governance/logistics problem
- one military or political victory that changes status by around chapter 20-30

Keep strategy practical:
- grain, troops, morale, intelligence, terrain, legitimacy
- avoid impossible omniscience unless the premise grants it
- make victories costly enough to preserve tension

### 都市脑洞
Mandatory elements:
- a hook that can be understood in one sentence
- a humiliating or constrained starting condition
- a public or semi-public first reversal
- continuous ladder of richer circles, harder opponents, or bigger social stakes
- at least one hidden-risk mechanism so the story is not only wish fulfillment

## Quality Bar
Before finalizing, silently check:
- can the opening hook be explained in one breath?
- does chapter 1 contain conflict, not just setup?
- does the first payoff arrive quickly enough?
- can the premise support at least 200 chapters conceptually?
- do the first 50 chapters avoid obvious repetition?
- is every 10-chapter block doing a different job?

If not, revise the outline before presenting it.

## Output Constraints
- Default to Chinese.
- Write like an experienced commercial web novel planner, not a theory essay.
- Be specific and decisive.
- Do not pad with generic writing advice unless the user asks for advice.
- Prefer concrete beats over abstract summaries.
- When multiple directions are plausible, present the strongest one first and keep alternatives brief.
